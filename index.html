Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the frequently asked questions

What is roadmap.sh?

▼

roadmap.sh is a community effort to create learning paths, guides, project ideas and other similar content to help developers grow in their careers.

What are the plans for roadmap.sh?

▼

We plan to expand roadmap.sh by adding more learning paths, interactive exercises, and community features to better serve developers at all stages of their careers.

How is roadmap.sh built?

▼

roadmap.sh is built using modern web technologies including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and various frameworks. The project is open source and hosted on GitHub.

Can I use roadmap.sh in my team?

▼

Yes, roadmap.sh is free to use for individuals and teams. We encourage teams to use our resources for onboarding, skill development, and career growth planning.

How can I create custom roadmaps?

▼

You can create custom roadmaps by forking our GitHub repository and modifying the existing roadmaps or creating new ones from scratch. We welcome community contributions!

Is roadmap.sh really 7th most starred project on GitHub?

▼

Yes, roadmap.sh is indeed one of the most starred projects on GitHub, consistently ranking among the top repositories. This reflects its popularity and value to the developer community.
